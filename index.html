CAPS Chemistry Curriculum Overview

The Grade 12 Chemistry curriculum follows the Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement (CAPS) framework, covering essential topics that build on Grade 10 and 11 knowledge.

Key Points & Tips:

1

Focus on understanding concepts rather than memorizing

2

Practice calculations regularly

3

Learn to interpret chemical equations and diagrams

4

Master the periodic table trends

5

Understand the relationship between theory and practical applications

Matric Chemistry Exam Structure

The Grade 12 Chemistry exam consists of two papers: Paper 1 (theoretical) and Paper 2 (practical and theory). Each paper is 3 hours long.

1

Paper 1: 150 marks, covers all theoretical aspects

2

Paper 2: 150 marks, includes practical investigations and theory

3

Both papers contribute equally to your final mark

4

Time management is crucial - practice under timed conditions

5

Read questions carefully and show all working for calculations
